Our Treatments

Combining preventive care and modern technology to deliver lifelong health and wellness.

Painless Root Canals

Your teeth deserve the best, especially when it is a treatment as intricate as the root canal. At CASA DENTIQUE Our skilled endodontists do the majority of root canal procedures within 30 minutes while always making sure you feel at ease.

Crowns

At CASA DENTIQUE dental restorations can be completed in just one visit with CEREC technology. With the help of this cutting-edge system, crowns can be perfectly designed, manufactured, and fitted in a single appointment.

Dental Implants

The Ideal Replacement for Missing Teeth. The best way to replace lost or damaged teeth with artificial fixtures that look and feel just like real teeth is with dental implants, which can be used to replace one, several, or all of your teeth.

Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeries

Maxillofacial surgeries can heal illnesses, treat wounds, or rectify flaws in your face, jaw, or mouth. At CASA DENTIQUE, a skilled dental surgeon performs the surgery which aids many in reducing discomfort, correcting abnormalities, and regaining function.

Full Mouth Rehabilitation

When the oral health fully declines resulting in numerous dental issues, one will require a full mouth reconstruction (rehabilitation). By erasing at least 10 to 20 years of facial aging, the technique has the ability to turn back the hands of time in addition to appearing and feeling younger.

Smile Designing

CASA DENTIQUE has always used cutting-edge dental technologies in its procedures, and is the leader in digital smile design (DSD). Through DSD our experts can create excellent, gorgeous smiles using this method which is also beneficial to general oral health.

Dentures

Complete or partial tooth loss averts you from eating & speaking properly. CASA DENTIQUE has several excellent choices for tooth replacement. When necessary, our dental clinic works in conjunction with knowledgeable and competent prosthodontists to offer our patients dentures.

Braces & Aligners

Braces and aligners are the preferred treatment for disorders such as uneven tooth alignment, jaw correction, better chewing, and smile attractiveness. Along with the greatest facilities for your dental care, the clinic has specialist consultants on staff to offer you the best solutions for any and all orthodontic demands you may have.

Bridges

A dental bridge can assist restore the appearance and functionality of your teeth if you have missing teeth, including your front teeth. It fills in the space left by the lost tooth. Also prevent the surrounding teeth from moving or shifting in the mouth.

What do you need to know regarding Medical visas?

Current restrictions control foreigners' entry, stay, and departure from India. By giving the necessary components, we will help you get a medical visa from the embassy or consulate of your nation. Obtaining a medical visa is strongly advised while going for treatment. The benefit of a medical visa is the ability to extend it if necessary, during treatment, this is something that is not feasible with a tourist visa.
